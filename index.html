Boulder Gaming Club

We are a group of fun-loving, like-minded people who love video games! We have all different kinds of members: console players, PC players, mobile players, avid gamers, and even just the occasional gamer. During our weekly meetings, we talk about games and gaming, plan events and competitions and, of course, game! Check out our page and contact us if you'd like to join!

Our Weekly Meetings

The Gaming Club meets every Tuesday from 8pm-9pm.


  • 8-8:05: Meet and greet, chat with members
  • 8:05-8:20: Introduce new members (if necessary). Discuss recent/upcoming events, questions, issues, etc.
  • 8:20-8:55: PLAY GAMES! Play with other members, try new games, compete in inter-club competitions.
  • 8:55-9: Wrap-up. Final remarks, reminders for upcoming events.

If you would like to attend a meeting, don't hesitate to send us an email for more information! Feel free to just show up at any meeting too!
