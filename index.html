Boulder Gaming Club

We are a group of fun-loving, like-minded people who love video games! We have all different kinds of members: console players, PC players, mobile players, avid gamers, and even just the occasional gamer. During our weekly meetings, we talk about games and gaming, plan events and competitions and, of course, game! Check out our page and contact us if you'd like to join!
